NLP in Metagenomics

August 9, 2022

General information: Application of NLP in metagenomics gene prediction. Next generation sequencing technologies applied in metagenomics results in numerous sequencing fragments coming from different species. The most fundamental aspect in metagonmics is the accurate identification of genes from metagenomics fragments. Deep learning approaches show promising potential for the gene identifications from metagenomics.

Approach:

  • Conclusion

Identification of genes directly from fragments of metagenomics data is an important aspect in annotating metagenomes. But, due to the absence of assembly of metagenomes and the incomplete and fragmented nature of the metagenomic sequencing data gene predictions are not accurate. Hence, there is a need for the development of computational methods for coding ORF identification from the short reads of metagenomics. In this study, we used NLP for gene predictions using a canine metageomics dataset. This generated network may be used for future projects relevant to gene predictinos in different species.
